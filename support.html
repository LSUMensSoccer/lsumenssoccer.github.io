Support the Club

The LSU Men's Club Soccer Team is a Sport Club within the University Recreation Department. Sport Club Teams at LSU are financially independent organizations and, in order to be a competitive team, LSU Men's Club Soccer has many financial obligations. Field rentals, travel, new uniforms, balls, are all regual expenses the team needs to incur in order to compete.

Despite active fundraising efforts throughout the year, much of this required financial burden falls on individual team members. Meeting these financial demands year after year speaks volumes about the dedication of Club members, and with your help we can ensure that future soccer talent at LSU can pursue a level of competition unhindered by finances.

Online Donation Instructions

STEP ONE

Under "Gift Donation" select "Choose a Fund(s)" and select "Other Funds"

STEP TWO

Fill out the "Donor Information" section in it's entirety

STEP THREE

Write LSU Men's Soccer Club in the "Comments" section and include a brief statement outlining the intent of the donation. Intent can be a general statement such as "support club activities"

Donate By Check

Please make your check payable to "LSU Foundation" with "LSU Men's Soccer Club" in the memo line, write a brief statement outlining the intent of the donation such as "support club activities", and mail to:
